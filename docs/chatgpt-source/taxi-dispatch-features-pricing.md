# Booking & Customer Features

## Online Booking Capture

Booking form fields include:

- Pickup address
- Destination
- Date
- Time
- Passenger count
- Contact details
- Account number (optional)

Pricing recalculates dynamically when form fields change.

---

## Account Pricing

Account customers may have different tariffs.

When an **account number is entered**, the system recalculates pricing based on account pricing rules.

# SaaS Pricing Models

Taxi Dispatch can support several pricing models.

## Model 1 – Per Driver Pricing

Monthly fee based on driver count.

Example:

- Up to 10 drivers – £99/month
- Up to 50 drivers – £299/month
- Unlimited drivers – £599/month

---

## Model 2 – Per Booking Pricing

Small fee per completed booking.

Example:

£0.30 – £1.00 per booking

Useful for companies with fluctuating demand.

---

## Model 3 – Website + Growth Platform

Tiered SaaS pricing.

Example:

Starter
- AI taxi website
- Booking capture
- Basic SEO pages

Growth
- AI SEO engine
- Analytics dashboard
- SMS integrations

Enterprise
- Dispatch integrations
- AI demand analytics
- Custom domains

---

## Model 4 – Hybrid Pricing

Base subscription plus booking fee.

Example:

£99/month + £0.50 per booking
